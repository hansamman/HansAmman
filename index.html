            <h2>About Hans Amman</h2>

     <img src="profile.jpg" alt="Hans Amman">

            <p>Hans Amman is a distinguished Professor of Computational Economics at the University of Amsterdam (UvA). With a career spanning over four decades, he has been a pioneer in the field of computational economics and a key figure in academic governance in the Netherlands.</p>

            <p>In 2025, he was appointed <strong>Officer in the Order of Orange-Nassau</strong> for his significant contributions to science and society. He has served as Executive Vice President for the University of Amsterdam, Utrecht University, and TU Eindhoven.</p>
